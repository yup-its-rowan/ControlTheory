Introduction to Pure Pursuit

The base of Pure Pursuit is an algorithm that dynamically computes current location and an intended path to manipulate a body’s movement into a unique, smoothed form of path following. Though there are many different ways of maintaining a smoothed path following algorithm, Pure Pursuit defines itself by creating a unique “look-ahead point” and then applying the correct vectors to move towards that point.
